Brownie in a Mug

  • Ingredients
  • Steps
  • Finished Product

This is the easiest brownie recipe ever! It only takes 5 minutes, a few simple ingredients, and one mug to make this delicious treat.

  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 2 TBSP of cocoa
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 2 TBSP of vegetable oil
  • 1/8 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream
  1. Add the dry ingredients to the mug and stir
  2. Add the wet ingredients and stir
  3. Put in microwave for 1 minute and 40 seconds
  4. Top it with a scoop of ice cream

Voila!
